Cognitive Dissonance is everywhere.

How do we navigate life while swimming through a sea of opposing forces all the time? Where is the balanced, sweet spot of existence that is necessary for sanity? We know that flexibility is key, change is the only constant, and reality is, well... relative.

How do we survive this hybrid world teetering between modernity and our universal yearning for a wilder and simpler life? Is there a way to apply earthly perspectives and spiritual cosmology at the same time?

Can we stay in that cognizant energy flow without perpetual substantive additions? I aim to explore and define this harmonious tension that is Cognitive Dissonance.
